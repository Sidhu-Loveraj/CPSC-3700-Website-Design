Frequently Asked Questions

How can I fund my account? ▼

You can fund your account through an Electronic ACH, wire, check, internal transfer or by depositing securities like stock or cryptocurrency.

Are my securities insured? ▼

Spark's insurance policy provided coverage on cash and securities up to $150 million, subject to a maximum limit of $40 million for any one customer's securities and $1 million for any one customer's cash.

How does Sparky the A.I work? ▼

Sparky utilizes a variety of metrics against a stock's and crypto's performance such as the relative strength index and moving averages to indicate if a stock is a good buy or sell.
